Our ability to create a significant marketing strategy in a short time frame was instrumental for the project’s launch. Elevate’s comprehensive strategy, involving community building, campaign creation, and influencer utilization, strengthened the project’s narrative and integrity.

Web3 Consulting

Challenge

In the early stages of collaborating with the Blueshift team, we encountered a small community with minimal engagement. Our mission was to establish an effective marketing strategy and develop both the community and Blueshift’s integrity from scratch. In a brief span of time, we created one of the most vibrant Cardano communities, whilst capturing attention far beyond the Cardano ecosystem.

Objectives

Played a key role in reshaping the marketing strategy, resulting in a robust project narrative and a thriving community
Garnered significant interest from the Cardano community and prominent influencers on the path to the project launch
