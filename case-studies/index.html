The Occam.Fi ecosystem is the first decentralized launchpad and DeFi ecosystem designed for the Cardano ecosystem. It is created to deliver market-leading launchpad capabilities, DEX tools, and liquidity pools. The Occam.fi ecosystem is managed and maintained by the Occam Association, a Switzerland-based entity operating from the crypto-friendly Canton of Zug.
