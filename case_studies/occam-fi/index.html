OccamFi

Case Study

Elevate was a crucial part of OccamFi between June 2021 and July 2022. We were instrumental in launching over 15 successful projects, handling tasks from developing launchpad marketing strategies to providing consultancy and incubation services in the field of marketing. Our expertise played a significant role in elevating Occam to its position as the premier launchpad in the Cardano ecosystem.

Challenge

Our partnership with Occam.Fi started with one project having been previously launched through the OccamRazer Launchpad. They have aimed to support the marketing demands of numerous projects seeking launchpad access. In a short period of time, we have established a strategy, enhanced our marketing capabilities, and directly participated in launching over 15 projects in less than a year.

Objectives

Developed a marketing strategy to ensure the flawless execution of multiple projects on the launchpad
Actively engaged in marketing and consulting, leading to the successful launch of over 15 projects 
Contributed and played a pivotal role in making Occam the foremost launchpad in the Cardano ecosystem
